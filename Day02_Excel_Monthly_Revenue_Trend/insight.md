## Day 2 — Excel Monthly Revenue Trend

**Dataset:** Superstore Sales (51,000+ rows)
**Question:** Which month had peak and lowest sales?
**Answer:** December is peak ($1,580,816) and 
February is lowest ($543,768)
**Key insight:** Sales follow a strong seasonality 
pattern — dipping early year and surging in Q4
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel
**Key functions:** TEXT(), SUMIF(), Line Chart
