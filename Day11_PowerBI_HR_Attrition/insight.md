## Day 11 — Power BI HR Attrition Dashboard

**Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics (1470 rows, 35 columns)
**Question:** Which department has highest turnover?

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Employees: 882
- Average Age: 36.65 years
- Average Monthly Income: $6.38K
- Average Years at Company: 6.86 years

**Insights:**
- Overall attrition rate: 17% (150 employees left)
- 83% employees stayed (732)
- Highest attrition: Research & Development dept
- Top attrition role: Sales Executive
- Top education field: Life Sciences

**Tool:** Power BI Desktop
**Visuals:** KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Pie Chart,
Donut Chart, Gender Filter
