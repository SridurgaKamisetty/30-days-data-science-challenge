## Day 9 — Excel Product Performance Pivot Table

**Dataset:** Supermarket Sales (1000 rows)
**Question:** Which product-city combo is most profitable?

**Insights found:**
- Top city: Naypyitaw — ₹5,265 gross income
- Top revenue product: Food & Beverages — ₹56,144
- Most units sold: Electronic Accessories — 971 units
- Best rated product: Food & Beverages — 7.11 rating
- Top combo: Health & Beauty in Naypyitaw — ₹1,131

**Tool:** Microsoft Excel
**Key features:** PivotTable, PivotChart, 
Clustered Bar Chart, Multiple Pivot Tables
